Our Vision

Change Community

Transforming the community from simply attending events to a productive and aware society striving for development in both hard and soft skills.

Qualifying Students

Equipping and qualifying students and connecting them to the job market through real-world applied projects and a deep, not superficial, technological culture.

Technology for Humanity

Focusing on the human and social aspects, not just the academic, in accordance with the IEEE spirit of “Technology for Humanity”.

Opportunities

Providing opportunities for all and creating a competitive internal environment that encourages excellence and innovation.

Active Members

Increasing the number of active members, because our success is not measured by the number of registered members, but by the people who actively participate and contribute.

Partnerships

Building partnerships with entities inside and outside the university to exchange expertise and provide members with new and diverse experiences.

Documenting

Documenting all work and activities professionally and honestly and establishing a work system that ensures the team's continuity after our administration ends.

Transforming

Transforming the community from simply attending events to a productive and aware society striving for development in both hard and soft skills.

Main Goal

The goal is to organize six technical workshops, conduct two training courses, hold two competitions, and arrange two events. In addition, at least two initiatives will be launched to further support engagement and development within the community.
